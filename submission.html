The word and latex template for preparing your submission can be obtained here. The instructions for manuscript preparation is available here. Each paper should have minimum 8 and maximum 10 pages.

After submission, each paper will go for a review process and the decision will be made based on the anonymous reviewers’ report. Please check the followings while preparing your manuscripts.

· All submitted manuscripts will be checked for the similarity through the Turnitin platform. Papers having similarity index more than 15% will be rejected without any further review.
· A paper should not have a similarity more than 3% of a single source.
· The text generated through AI and LLM will be checked through Turnitin and the papers having more than 5% text generated through any AI tool will be rejected.
