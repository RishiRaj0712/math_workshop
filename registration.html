Registration Fee Details for Participants

• Students (UG, PG, PhD)--- INR 3000/- including GST.
• Faculty Members and Postdoctoral Fellow --- INR 6000/- including GST.
• Participants from Abroad --- USD 200 including GST.


Note 1 : Upon completion of the registration process, the final list of participants eligible for the workshop will be released. Only those participants will be required to pay the registration fee.

Note 2: The workshop fee includes accommodation, registration kit, breakfast and lunches, dinner(s) , gala dinner and tea/ coffee during breaks.

* For more information regarding accommodation, kindly refer to Accommodation Page.

How to submit registration fee:

Using Bank transfer

Please transfer the respective amount in the IITR conference account (ACCOUNT DETAILS). After transfer the amount, please send the receipt by email to the "indofrench@ma.iitr.ac.in".

Using SBI Collect

  1. Please open the https://www.onlinesbi.com/sbicollect/icollecthome.html in your browser. If clicking the link doesn’t work, copy the link and paste it into the browser.
  2. Fill the Workshop Code: WORKSHOP-151-MTD
  3. Select Category: Individual (or Corporate if making payment as Sponsor/ Partner)
  4. Please enter your Name, Email ID.
  5. Please enter the payable amount.
  6. Please enter remarks, if any.
  7. Again enter Name, DOB, Mobile Number, Email ID , and the text as shown in the image.
  8. Click Submit button to continue.
  9. Verify the details and Click Confirm button to continue.
  10. You will be directed to a payment gateway where you can choose from a range of options.
  11. After making the payment, you will get a confirmation page and an SMS on the mobile number provided above.
  12. Note down SBCollect Reference Number on the confirmation page.
  13. Email the payment receipt to the conference email ID indofrench@ma.iitr.ac.in.

Using QR Code

Please transfer the respective amount through the QR Code of the IITR conference account.
