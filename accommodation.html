IIT Roorkee strongly believes in the old Indian adage "Athithi Devo Bhava". We give our visitors the best facilities be it in terms of service, ambience or food. The campus has link guest houses to accommodate the guests and delegates who visit the campus.


*For student participants, accommodation will be provided on sharing basis in the guest rooms of hostels within the campus at free of cost.

*For Faculty participants, accommodation will be chargeable in the guest house available in the campus according to the availability.


Institute Guest House

It is one of the visitors' Guest house present in the Campus. It offers world-class facilities with a committee room and dining hall. All the rooms are air-conditioned and are well furnished. In each of them, two furnished beds are provided. Each of them has an attached toilet with hot and cold water facility. All rooms are serviced by an emergency generator set. All rooms are also provided with colour televisions with Cable connection.
